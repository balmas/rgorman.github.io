Selected Relation Labels for Syntax Assignment

  • MnVb: main verb
  • Sbj: subject of verb
  • Pnom: predicate nominal with sum, etc.
  • ATR: attributive adjective or attributive genitive
  • ADV: adverbial (same as adverb--for now)
  • Prep: preposition
  • Oprep: object of preposition

  • Accusative
    • DirObj: direct object of verb

  • Dative
    • IndObj: indirect object of verb
    • Ref: reference specifying adjective
    • Compl: dative complement of verb

  • Ablative
    • Compl: ablative complement of verb
    • TWhen: time at which (time when)

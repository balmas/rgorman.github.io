8

  • castrīs: neuter
  • proficīscuntur: proficīscor, proficīscī, profectus sum
9
eōs armātī ex praesidiō secūtī sunt.
  • armātī: armō, armāre, armāvī, armātus; the adjectival qualities of the perfect participle allow it to function as a substantive, = "armed [ones]/armed men"
  • praesidiō: neuter
  • secūtī sunt: sequor, sequī, secūtus sum
10
trēs lēgātī Athēnās missī sunt.
  • lēgātī: masculine
  • Athēnās: ACC X without a preposition can = "to X" when X is the name of a city
  • missī sunt: mittō, mittere, mīsī, missus
11
in urbe ā Catilīnā relictī sunt.
  • urbe: feminine
  • ā: a form of the preposition ab, + ABL X may = "by X" when the relevant verb is passive
  • Catilīnā: PrN, Catilīna, Catilīnae, m.
  • relictī sunt: relinquō, relinquere, relīquī, relictus
12
ei litterae ā Gādibus redduntur.
  • ei: see is, ea, id in "Adjectives 3"
  • litterae: feminine
  • ā: preposition + ABL X = "from X"
  • Gādibus: pLN of a city in Spain, Gādēs, Gādium, f. plural
  • redduntur: reddō, reddere, reddidī, redditus
13
dēfertur eius sermō ad Aprōnium.
  • dēfertur: dēferō, dēferre, dētulī, dēlātus; dēfertur is present passive 3rd singular; it is irregular for the expected dēferitur
  • sermō: masculine
  • Aprōnium: PrN, Aprōnius, Aprōnī, m.
14

15

  • impetus: masculine
  • factus est: faciō, facere, fēcī, factus
16
hōc tempore data est Eumenī Cappadocia.
  • tempore: ABL X without a preposition = "at/in/on X" when X is a time
  • data est: dō, dare, dedī, datus; verb frame: SBJ gives ACC X to DAT Y is reduced to SBJ X is given to DAT Y when verb is passive.
  • Eumenī: PrN of a Greek general, Eumenēs, Eumenis, m.
  • Cappadocia: pLN of a region in Asia Minor, Cappadocia, Cappadociae, f.

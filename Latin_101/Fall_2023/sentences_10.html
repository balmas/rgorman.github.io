Translate these sentences into English and be ready to explain your work.

1
Carthāginem īre iussī sunt.
  • Carthāginem: pLN of a city in Africa, Carthāgō, Carthāginis, f. = "Carthage"; ACC X without a preposition can = "to X" when X is the name of a city
  • īre: eō, īre, īvī, ____, = "go"
  • iussī sunt: iubeō, iubēre, iussī, iussus = "order/command"; verb frame: SBJ orders ACC X to infinitive Y. In the passive, X becomes SBJ (nom) and Y remains an infinitive.
2
mē Pompeius Capuam īre voluit.
  • mē: SBJ of an infinitive is usually ACC
  • Pompeius: PrN, Pompeius, Pompeī, m.
  • Capuam: pLN of a town in Italy, Capua, Capuae, f.
  • īre: eō, īre, īvī, ____, = "go"
  • voluit: volō, velle, voluī, ____, = "wish/want"; verb frame: SBJ wants X, where X is an infinitive clause
3
tē tamen sollicitum esse nōlō.
  • tē: SBJ of an infinitive is usually ACC
  • esse: sum, esse, fuī, ____; verb frame: SBJ is PNOM (PNOM is same case as SBJ)
  • nōlō: nōlō, nolle, nōluī; verb frame: SBJ does not want X, where X in an infinitive clause
4
volō et esse et habērī grātus.
  • volō: volō, velle, voluī, ____, = "wish/want"; verb frame: SBJ wants X, where X is an infinitive clause
  • et ... et: = "both ... and"
  • esse: sum, esse, fuī, ____
  • habērī: habeō, habēre, habuī, habitus: = "hold/consider"; in the passive the verb frame is SBJ is considered PNOM (PNOM same case as SBJ)
5
statuērunt bellī facere fīnem.
  • statuērunt: statuō, statuere, statuī, statūtus; verb fram: SBJ decides to X, where X is an infinitive clause
  • bellī: neuter
  • facere, faciō, facere, fēcī, factus
  • fīnem: masculine
6
et bonī et beātī volumus esse.
  • et ... et: = "both ... and"
  • bonī: bonus, bona, bonum = "good"
  • beātī: beātus, beāta, beātum = "happy/blessed"
  • volumus: see "Special verbs 1"; verb frame: SBJ wants X, where X is an infinitive clause
  • esse: sum, esse, fuī, ____; verb frame: SBJ is PNOM (PNOM usually same case as SBJ)
7
pugnāre contrā mē īnstituistis.
  • pugnāre: pugnō, pugnāre, pugnāvī, pugnātus
  • contrā: preposition + ACC X = "against X"
  • īnstituistis: īnstituō, īnstituere, īnstituī, īnstitūtus; verb frame: SBJ decides to X, where X is an infinitive clause
8
Sīlius tē cum Clōdiō loquī vult.
  • Sīlius: PrN, Sīlius, Sīlī, m.
  • tē: ACC can be SBJ of an infinitive
  • cum: preposition + ABL X = "with X"
  • Clōdiō: PrN, Clōdius, Clōdī, m.
  • loquī: loquor, loquī, locūtus sum
  • vult: see "Special verbs 1"; verb frame: SBJ wants X, where X is an infinitive clause
9
equitum partem sē sequī iussit.
  • equitum: masculine
  • partem: feminine
  • sequī: sequor, sequī, secūtus sum
  • iussit: iubeō, iubēre, iussī, iussus = "order/command"; verb frame: SBJ orders ACC X to infinitive Y.

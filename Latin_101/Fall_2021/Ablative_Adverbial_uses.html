Adverbial uses of the ablative case WITHOUT A PREPOSITION (after Panhuis & Dickinson College Commentaries)

Separation/origin:

  1. curta vāsa fenestrīs cadunt. (The broken vessels fall from the windows)
  2. ego tuō sanguine ortus sum (I was born from your blood)
  3. Brundisiō profectī sumus (we set out from Brindisium)

Distance:

  1. hostēs magnīs intervallīs sequuntur (enemies follow at great intervals)
  2. mīlibus passum sex ā Caesaris castrīs cōnsedit (he takes a position six miles [six thousands of paces] from Caesar’s camp)

Cause/reason:

  • multī moriuntur terrōre (many die from terror)

Comparison:

  • lūce clariōra sunt tua cōnsilia (your plans are clearer than daylight)

Place:

  • erat Athēnīs spatiōsa domus (in Athens there was a large house)

Time:

  • in fīnis Vocontōrum diē septimō pervēnit (he arrived in the territory on the Vocontii on the seventh day)

Limitation/restriction/specification

  1. Helvētii reliquōs Gallōs virtūte praecūdunt (the Helvetii surpass the other Gauls in bravery)
  2. maiōrēs nātū (greater by birth [i.e. older])

Means/instrument

  1. opportūna loca armātīs hominibus obsidet (he occupies strategic places with armed people)
  2. multae arbōrēs meā manū sunt satae (Many trees were planted by my hand)

Accompaniement:

  • Subsequēbātur omnibus cōpiīs (He was following close with all the forces)

Manner:

  1. incrēdibilī celeritāte ad flūmen dēcurrunt (They run down to the river with incredible speed)
  2. ventī terrās turbine perflant (The winds blow through the lands in a whirl)

Road/route by which:

  1. mediō līmite currit (He runs on the middle track)
  2. Aurēliā Viā profectus est (He set out by the Aurelian Way)

Price/value:

  • Agrum vendidit sestertium sex mīlibus (He sold the land for 6000 sesterces.)

Description:

  1. Catalina fuit magna vi animi (lit.: Catalina was with great force of mind; i.e., Cataline had great force of mind)
  2. Claudia semone erat lepido (lit.: Claudia was with charming conversation; i.e., Claudia had a charming way of speaking)

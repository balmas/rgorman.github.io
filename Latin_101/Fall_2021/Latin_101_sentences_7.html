Translation Sentences 7

1
adhūc nōn vēnērunt.
The presence of adhūc indicates that we should translate vēnērunt as completed/perfective present.
2
adhūc nōn est profectus.
adhūc is important information; see the note to sentence 1.
3
fīlius ad mē nōn vēnerat.
vēnerat is completed past because it is what form?
4
in ea loca erat dēductus.
erat dēductus together are Pluperfect. What does that mean?
5
Alexandrī fuerant amīcī.
Alexandrī is Mas/Sing/Gen.
6
haec causa est eius modī.
eius modī together function as a predicate nominal. We will discuss.
7
heri enim vesperī vēnerat.
Notice the two time adverbs.
8
mihi in mentem nōn vēnerat.
in mentem venīre is how you say "come to mind" in Latin. The person whose mind is involved is referred to with the DAT as mihi here.
9
sunt Rōmae lēgātī Mīlēsiī.
Rōmae is not Genitive but rather Locative.
10
illī autem nōndum vēnerant.
vēnerant is Pluperfect.
11
is tum rēgis rēbus praeerat.
praeerat is Imperfect. rēbus is DAT; look at the verb frame in the vocab to see why.
12
praesidiō hostium tenēbātur.
praesidiō is ABL of instrument/means.
13
praesidiō eum locum tenēbant.
praesidiō is ABL of instrument/means.
14
cōpiās nōn ēdūcēbant in aciem.
ēdūcēbant is Imperfect (notice the -ba-)
15
multās iam prōvinciās occupāvit.
iam indicates that occupāvit is best translated as a Completed Present.
16
Appius ā Caesare nōndum redierat.
redierat is Pluperfect.
17
anteā cum Fabiō et Pediō fuerant.
fuerant is Pluperfect.
18
habet is in prōvinciā tuā negōtia.
Not too difficult.
19
Brūtō tuae litterae grātae sunt.
grātae is PNOM (predicate nominal). Brūtō is DAT of beneficiary.
20
Rhodiīs nāvibus praeerat Euphrānor.
praeerat is Imperfect. To understand why Rhodiīs nāvibus is DAT see the verb frame information for praesum.
21
erat eō tempore Antōnius Brundisī.
erat is Imperfect; Brundisī is LOC.
22
mīlia enim vīgintī in armīs habēbat.
vīgintī is Neu/Pl/Acc and is an attribute of mīlia. habēbat is Imperfect.
23
summam inter eōs habet auctōritātem.
Not much to say here.
24
eō tempore tribūnus mīlitum fuerat.
mīlitum is Mas/Pl/Gen of pattern 3c. fuerat is Pluperfect.

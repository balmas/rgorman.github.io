Hoc legat et lectō carmine doctus amet.

  • quis: = "anyone"
  • amandī: gerund, sing. neut. gen. = "loving"
  • lectō carmine: best as ABL absolute
  • doctus: a secondary predication; this structure is often best rendered into English with "as"

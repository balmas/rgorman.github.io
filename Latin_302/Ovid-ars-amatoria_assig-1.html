xx

Text

Sī quis in hōc artem populō nōn nōvit amandī, Hoc legat et lectō carmine doctus amet. Arte citae vēlōque ratēs rēmōque moventur, Arte levēs currūs: arte regendus amor. Curribus Automedōn lentīsque erat aptus habēnīs,      5 Tīphys in Haemoniā puppe magister erat:

Notes

l. 2: doctus is a secondary predication. It does not so much specify the subject as it modifies amet. It can be rendered something like "as a learned man."

l. 3: vēlum, vēlī, neu. A sail.

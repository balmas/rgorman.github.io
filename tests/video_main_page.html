Videos: Making Greek Trees

If you know Greek already and would like to make dependency syntax trees, I have recorded some videos to help get you started.
I walk you through how to download a text into Perseids' treebanking platform (Arethusa), show you what all the labeling means, teach you the conventions of hanging Greek trees, and demonstrate how to perform minor editing on the text itself within the .xml file.

The following videos are available on YouTube:

  • Getting Started Making Greek Trees (32:43)
  • Simple Sentences: Treeing Xenophon, Hellenica 1, sentences 2-5 (34:27)
  • Intermediate Sentences: Xenophon, Hellenica 1, sentences 22, 26-28 (41:09)
  • Making Appositives, External Dependencies, and Verbal Attributes (APP, ExD, ATV)
  • Simple Editing within Arethusa/Perseids: Xenophon, Hellenica 1, sentence 145
  • Complex Sentences: Xenophon, Hellenica 1, sentence 145
